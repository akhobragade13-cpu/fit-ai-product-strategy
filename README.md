# fit.ai: Behavioral Fitness Infrastructure

**fit.ai** is an AI-driven behavioral fitness infrastructure that transforms **5-minute micro-workouts** into a compounding daily asset. Unlike traditional fitness apps that demand "heroic efforts" and contribute to early burnout, fit.ai bridges the **82% Intention-Behavior Gap** by legitimising small, daily efforts as the foundation of a long-term **"Daily Mover" identity**.

## 🛑 The Problem: The Adherence Crisis
Traditional fitness models are failing because they ignore the primary psychological and logistical barriers to health:

*   **The 82% Intention-Behavior Gap:** Research indicates that the vast majority of exercise behavior variance is unexplained by intention alone; most people want to work out but lack the infrastructure to execute.
*   **The 67% "Ramp-Period Crisis":** Two-thirds of dropouts occur during the initial weeks because programs are too "heavy" or intense before a user even forms a habit.
*   **Perceived "Time Poverty":** Cited by **40% of users** as their #1 barrier, the current 45-60 minute workout model is fundamentally incompatible with modern life.
*   **The Abstinence Violation Effect:** An "all-or-nothing" mindset where a single missed session due to work or life leads to a total shame-driven habit collapse.

---

## 🚀 The Solution: Adaptive Adherence
fit.ai replaces the rigid schedule with a **lapse-resilient behavioral engine** powered by agentic AI.

### **Core MVP Features**
1.  **5-10 Minute Micro-Workout Engine:** High-value, low-friction sessions designed to fit into "stolen minutes" between meetings or childcare.
2.  **Adaptive Adherence Engine (AI):** A real-time coach using **Predictive Risk Detection** to identify high-risk dropout windows and suggest **2-minute "Coping Plans"** to keep the habit loop alive.
3.  **"Time Math" Compounding Dashboard:** Visualizes how **5 minutes daily compounds into 30+ hours of fitness per year**, reframing exercise as a high-ROI asset.
4.  **Shallow Progression Enforcer:** To survive the 67% ramp-period dropout wall, the AI enforces an intentional **"intensity ceiling"** for the first 14–21 days to prioritize "showing up" over physical load.
5.  **Multi-Metric Progress (NSVs):** Prevents "False Plateau Despair" by tracking **Non-Scale Victories** like energy, sleep, and mood alongside weight.

---

## 📈 North Star Metric: Weekly Consistency Score (WCS)
We measure success by **frequency over intensity**. The **WCS** tracks the percentage of users completing at least 5 minutes of movement on **5 or more days** in a rolling 7-day period.

*   **Goal:** 25% 30-day retention on premium tiers (1.6x the industry average).
*   **Identity Shift:** Leveraging the **r=0.55 correlation** between habit strength and exercise identity.

## 👤 Target Segments
The product is strategically optimized for the most "helpable and profitable" 40% of the market:
*   **Busy Professionals (18-45 yrs):** Urban, high-income individuals seeking "Fitness ROI" and calendar integration.
*   **Working Mothers (25-45 yrs):** Juggling professional and domestic roles; they comprise **60% of fitness app users** and value privacy and lapse-resilience.
